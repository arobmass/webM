Art is defined by vague terms: line-weight, atmospheric perspective, color, and composistion. Yet, the greatest aspect of all art--the aspect that screams, "Hey, this looks familiar"-- is a result of time. Time, the time in which a piece was made, impacts the importance of art the most. Without refrence to time, art may be useless.
